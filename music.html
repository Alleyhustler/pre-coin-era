welcome to my music page!

I started making music in late 2023 on SoundTrap, after remembering a time I experimented with music making programs back in middle school. After some convincing from my friends, I started uploading my songs onto YouTube starting in March 2024. I really enjoy making and sharing music online!

My music falls in the vaporwave category, more specifically synthwave and lofiwave with some retrowave on the side. Click on the albums below to browse and listen to my songs, and I hope y'all enjoy!
