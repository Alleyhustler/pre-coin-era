jude's song of the week ♥

(8/1) week 11: [alien alien] by nayutalieN



i got addicted to project diva again recently, and i think i've played this song more than any other so far in the game's massive library. as a result, it's been playing in my head non-stop this past week.





(7/28) week 10: [inspire] by ayumi hamasaki



i think we could all use some inspiration from ayu right about now. i know her general discography inspired high school me struggling with depression --





(7/21) week 9: [the future] by fujifire



not only is this song a vibe, but also one of the many inspirations for my own music! this song also gives me ratiorine vibes. like ratiorine having a night of steamy passion in penacony with aideen park right outside their hotel window vibes --




(7/14) week 8: [emotions] by desired



a very solid contender for top 3 greatest vaporwave song of all time. in fact, i'm pretty sure this song would be #1 if it wasn't for the true GOAT, skylar spence. still a 10/10 song, though.





(7/7) week 7: [oil ocean (remix)] by Funk Fiction



i've been listening to this banger remix of the classic sonic 2 level song for the past like week. it's so damn good, along with the other sonic remixes done by funk fiction! out of all the songs in the sonic & chill album though, their oil ocean remix in particular is especially satisfying to listen to~




(6/30) week 6: [take me] by riamuMAZE1981



probably the ideal song to listen to while walking around a big city during the night. also an ideal song to listen to just in general.






(6/23) week 5: [skylar spence] by saint pepsi



the undisputed goat of music. music peaked when saint pepsi dropped this absolute banger. this song is so good in fact he would later change his musician name to skylar spence.





(6/16) week 4: [fly with me] by yung bae



if my life was a generic 90's sitcom, this song would play in the background whenever i'm daydreaming about my ideal future living a peaceful and happy life by the ocean.





(6/9) week 3: [soda city funk] by RAMDARAM


banger song that is single-handedly responsible for me falling down the vaporwave rabbit hole. basically if it wasn't for this song then i probably wouldn't be so into vaporwave today.




(6/1) week 2: [heavenly star] by genki rockets


this song has SUCH a special place in my heart. genki rockets in general has a special place in my heart; i love every one of their songs but heavenly star sticks out from the rest due to it being the first song by them i listened to. also shout out to No More Heroes (goated game) for introducing me to Genki Rockets (and this song)




(5/26) week 1: [suisei groove] by mikazuki BIGWAVE


out of all of the bangers put out by mikazuki BIGWAVE, suisei groove is without a doubt my favorite! it never fails to put me in a good mood (or make my mood even better) every time i listen to it. the beat is sooo catchy!
